ABOUT US

Calvary Chapel

Calvary Chapel Bible College Nepal was established in 2015 and is an Evangelical Training Centre based in Kathmandu but operating in many regions of Nepal (and online). Our mission is to disciple students and leaders into mature followers of Christ and to equip them for ministry. We exist to support the churches of Nepal by training current and future leaders and supporting them to develop and plant churches that are shaped by God's Word.
